# TAP (Task and Progress Tracker)

TAP is a powerful and engaging task management and progress tracking application designed to help users set, track, and complete their daily and occasional goals — while boosting productivity, motivation, and health. 

With features like AI-driven task suggestions, reminders, streaks, gamification, and progress visualization, TAP turns goal tracking into a rewarding experience.

---

## ✨ Features

✅ **User Task Section**  
- Add personal tasks categorized as **daily** or **occasional**  
- Set priority levels: High, Medium, Low  

✅ **App-Suggested Tasks**  
- AI-suggested tasks based on health, fitness, or productivity  
- Complete tasks to earn points and maintain streaks

✅ **Progress Tracking**  
- Visualize progress with **graphs** and **pie charts** (weekly & monthly)  

✅ **Gamification & Rewards**  
- Earn points, badges, and levels for consistency  
- Build and maintain streaks for daily task completion

✅ **Community & Social Features**  
- Leaderboard to compare streaks and points with other users  
- Challenge friends and share achievements

✅ **AI Personalization (future integration)**  
- Personalized suggestions based on user habits or health data (Google Fit integration planned)

---

## 💻 Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** raect.js typescript
- **Backend:** supabase  

## 📱 How It Works

1. Add your tasks → daily or occasional  
2. Set reminders and priority  
3. Get AI-suggested tasks  
4. Complete tasks to earn points and streaks  
5. Track your progress and stay motivated  
6. Compete with friends and rise on the leaderboard!
